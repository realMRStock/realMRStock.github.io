About Me

Aloha, I am a postdoctoral researcher in astroparticle physics at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa. My research centers on detector response, energy scale, and optical calibration in large-scale neutrino and dark matter experiments, including KamLAND-Zen and DarkSide, bridging instrumentation and physics analysis in low-background detectors.
